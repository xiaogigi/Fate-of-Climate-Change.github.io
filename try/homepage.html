Seattle’s Street Eco-Terrorism
Seattle’s Street of Dreams is now a street of nightmares. The collection of multi-million dollar luxury homes was recently burned to the ground by people claiming to be eco-terrorists. Eco-terror, to paraphrase the FBI, is defined as the use of violence against victims or property by an environmentally-oriented group of environmental-political reasons. The FBI considers environmental terrorism to be the NO.1 domestic terror threat facing the U.S.

Some of the experts think the rising of eco-terrorists is resulted by the frequent eco-awareness campaign. They explained that with the increasing attention towards climate change, organizations with a vicious purpose can incite people easily using climate change.
100% Transport Eletrificaiton in Shenzhen
In 30 years, Shenzhen has became the first city to electrify 100% of its public transport. Back to 2018, Shenzhen had already succeeded to replace all their public buses with electric buses. Following this green transit revolution, Shenzhen moved on to make all its taxis go electric, and their success of this action becomes an inspiration for the rest of world. Experts say:”The kind of sweeping changes that Shenzhen pulled off won’t come to U.S. cities without full national support and buy-in from local transit agencies.”
